Slow Cooker Chili

Description

A classic beef chili made to be cooked in a slow cooker.

Ingredients

  • 450g ground beef
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large can (28 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can kidney beans with liquid
  • 1 can black beans with liquid
  • 1 beef OXO envelope or other boullion/stock
  • 1 tbsp chili powder (or more if needed)
  • Chili flakes or other ground hot pepper to taste (optional)
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. In large saucepan on medium-high, add onions and sweat.
  2. When the onions are semi translucent, add ground beef, minced garlic and thyme, raising the heat a little to brown both the onions and the beef. While that is cooking add diced tomatoes, kidney and black beans to slow cooker.
  3. When beef is browned, add diced tomatoes, kidney beans and black beans (both with liquid). Add OXO envelope, chili powder, salt and pepper. Adjust chili powder and salt to taste. Set cooker to the appropriate time. Adjust chili powder and hot peppers to taste.
  4. Enjoy!
